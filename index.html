About Plunge

Plunge is a trusted, locally-owned plumbing company serving Southern California communities with integrity, reliability, and expert craftsmanship. We've been fixing pipes and solving plumbing problems for homeowners and businesses for over a decade.

Whether it's an emergency pipe burst, a routine maintenance call, or a complete bathroom remodel, our licensed and insured plumbers treat every job with the same attention to detail and respect for your home. We show up on time, we respect your space, and we stand behind our work.

When you call Plunge, you're not reaching a call center — you're reaching local plumbers who live and work in your community. We're available 7 days a week, with emergency response 24/7. Same-day service is our standard, not a luxury.
